About the Stock Performance Tracker

This app allows you to track the performance of stocks, indices, and mutual funds with interactive charts and detailed financial data. You can also download the data as an Excel file for further analysis.

Features:

  • Track stock prices, sector, industry, and more.
  • View 1-year historical performance charts.
  • Download financial data as an Excel file.
  • Stay updated with real-time stock data.
